### Unit 5 – R 1.3
### Energy from Fuel Project

In preparation for a mock international summit on energy, you will work individually or in pairs to research and prepare a project on R 1.3: Energy from Fuel (combustion, fossil fuels, biofuels, fuel cells).

***GUIDING QUESTION:***
***WHAT ARE THE CHALLENGES OF USING CHEMICAL ENERGY TO ADDRESS OUR ENERGY NEEDS?***

The product can be in any format (poster, website, presentation, etc. Be creative!) Each part (combustion, fossil fuels, biofuels, fuel cells) should include <u>at least</u>:
- 2 to 3 paragraphs of informational text
- 3 formative check questions
- 1 additional research source for those who would like to read more
- 1 related graphic
- 1 related video (optional)

You will then be selected to defend one of the following cases using your research:
1-What are the challenges of the burning of fossil to address our energy needs? Why should fossil fuel alternatives be used? Always cite the text when making an argument.
2-How can biofuels address our energy needs, and what are the challenges of using this fossil fuel alternative? Always cite the text when making an argument.
3-How can fuel cells address our energy needs, and what are the challenges of using this fossil fuel alternative? Always cite the text when making an argument.

You will be graded on the product AND on your presentation as per rubric.

As homework, you write a short paragraph discussing all of the above questions. Consider all evidence presented at the summit along with knowledge gained through your own research as you weigh pros and cons of tackling each energy source.
